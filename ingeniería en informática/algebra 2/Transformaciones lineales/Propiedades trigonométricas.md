# Definición
---
 * $cos(a)^2 + sin(a)^2 = 1$
 * $cos(a \pm b) = cos(a)cos(b) \mp sin(a)sin(b)$
 * $sin(a \pm b) = sin(a)cos(b) \pm cos(a)sin(b)$
 * $A ~ cos(\omega t) + B ~ sen(\omega t) = \sqrt{A^2 + B^2} ~ cos\left(\omega t - tan^{-1}\left( \frac{B}{A} \right)\right)$
