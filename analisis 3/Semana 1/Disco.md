#### Datos adicionales
$$D(z_0, \infty) = \mathbb{C}$$
$$D(z_0, 0) = \emptyset$$
$$\overline{D}(z_0,\infty) = \mathbb{C}$$
$$\overline{D}(z_0, 0) = \{z_0\}$$
$$D_0(z_0, \infty) = \mathbb{C} - \{z_0\}$$
