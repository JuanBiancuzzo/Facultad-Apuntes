# Definición
---
Dada la [[Serie]] $\bigg( \sum_{k = 0}^n |a_k| \bigg)_{n = 0}^\infty$ converge, entonces $\bigg( \sum_{k = 0}^n a_k \bigg)_{n = 0}^\infty$ converge. No vale la reciproca
